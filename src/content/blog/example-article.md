## Why infrastructure matters

AI systems now mediate everyday communication. This shifts how trust, context, and accountability travel through networks.

## Research notes

- Infrastructures are often invisible until they fail.
- Friction shapes what becomes visible in public discourse.
- Design choices in AI tools become communicative norms.

## Next questions

How do users calibrate trust when AI systems summarize, translate, or recommend? What happens to nuance in public debate?
